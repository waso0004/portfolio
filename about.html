Design Focus

My design philosophy is clean, minimal, and functional. I aim to remove anything that does not help the user, so the content and actions feel obvious. I focus on strong hierarchy, consistent spacing, and simple navigation so the experience stays clear across devices. I also design with accessibility in mind, using readable type, clear contrast, and comfortable tap targets.

Core Value

I do this work because I love seeing an experience improve with every iteration. I enjoy finding the small details that slow users down, fixing them, and watching the product feel smoother and more polished. I care about design that looks beautiful, but stays functional first, with clear choices, consistent patterns, and thoughtful details that make the experience feel easy and intentional.
